Kachin

The Kachin people live mainly in northern Myanmar and are known for their colorful traditions and unity festivals.

Content

  • Culture and Traditions
  • Traditional Dress

Culture and Traditions

Kachin culture is best represented by the famous Manau festival, marked by music, dance, and towering Manau poles.

They emphasize kinship, hospitality, and respect for nature, with traditions blending animism and Christianity.

Traditional Dress

Kachin attire is colorful, with men wearing jackets, sashes, and turbans, while women wear handwoven skirts and blouses.

Each design reflects clan heritage and is proudly worn at ceremonies and festivals.
